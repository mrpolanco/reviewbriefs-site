Common questions

What review sources do you work with?

Google, Booking.com, TripAdvisor, and Expedia. You can paste reviews directly into the form or share review page URLs. If you use a different platform, reach out and we'll let you know if we can accommodate it.

How many reviews do I need to submit?

We can work with as few as 20 reviews, though 50–100 produces the most reliable patterns. We'll analyze what you provide and flag if the sample size limits any findings.

How is the report delivered?

You'll receive a private web link (like this sample report) that you can share directly with clients, plus a PDF-optimized version you can save or print for presentations.

Do you use automated tools to generate reports?

We use a combination of review reading and structured analysis. The insights and copy in every report are reviewed and refined by a person before delivery — not sent directly from an automated pipeline.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes. If the report doesn't meet the standard of the sample report you saw before purchasing, we'll work with you to revise it or offer a full refund. We're a small team building trust — we'd rather get it right.

Is this a subscription?

The one-time brief is a single purchase — no subscription. The Agency Pilot is a monthly plan that cancels anytime.
