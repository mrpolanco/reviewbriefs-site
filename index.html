What you get

Everything your clients need to know,
in one document.

Each ReviewBriefs report distills hundreds of guest reviews into eight focused sections — no fluff, no dashboards to learn, no data science degree required.

Executive Summary

A clear one-page overview of reputation health, top trends, and the one thing to fix first — ready to drop into a client deck.

Top Praise Themes

The specific experiences guests rave about, ranked by frequency — so you know exactly what to double down on and amplify.

Reputation Risks

Patterns that are quietly damaging bookings — the recurring complaints that guests share online but rarely tell the front desk.

Guest Language Worth Reusing

The exact phrases real guests use to describe the property — the most authentic copy you'll ever have for ads, listings, and social.

Marketing Opportunities

Concrete channel-by-channel suggestions — what to promote, where to say it, and which guest segments are the most vocal advocates.

Priority Action List

A ranked table of actions by expected impact — with suggested owners and timelines so the report can move straight to a team meeting.

The process

Three steps from reviews
to ready-to-present report.

Share your reviews

Paste in up to 100 recent reviews from Google, Booking.com, TripAdvisor, or Expedia — or share the review page URLs and we'll handle the rest.

We read the patterns

Our review analysts read, categorize, and surface the themes that matter — praise patterns, friction points, and language signals worth capturing.

Receive your brief
