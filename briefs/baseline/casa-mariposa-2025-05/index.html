Top Risk Signals

Risk Signal
Possible Wi-Fi room cluster

In the sample report data, Wi-Fi issues appear as a recurring signal and seem concentrated in a specific room cluster. This looks like the highest-impact fixable operational friction to validate.

Risk Signal
Hot water inconsistency

Hot water complaints appear in the reviewed sample and may be concentrated in a small room group. This is a practical issue, but it can quickly outweigh praise when it affects a guest stay.

Risk Signal
Weekend noise and arrival clarity

Street-facing room noise and small arrival/parking confusion appear as secondary friction. Better expectation-setting could reduce avoidable disappointment.

Quick Wins to Investigate

  • Check Wi-Fi coverage in the affected room cluster. The reviewed sample suggests this issue has a disproportionate effect on lower ratings from otherwise interested guests.
  • Audit hot water reliability in rooms flagged by guest mentions. A focused maintenance check could remove one of the clearest sources of low-review risk.
  • Add parking, arrival, and street-facing room guidance to pre-arrival messages. This would help guests make better room choices and reduce preventable expectation gaps.
