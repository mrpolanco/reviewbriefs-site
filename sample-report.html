What's inside every brief
1

Executive Summary

A concise overview of reputation health: overall sentiment breakdown, the top three strengths, and the single highest-priority thing to fix. Designed to drop directly into a client presentation.

2

Top Praise Themes

Ranked by frequency of unprompted mention. Each theme includes supporting guest quotes, a frequency bar, and guidance on how to use the insight.

3

Reputation Risks

Recurring friction points - prioritized by frequency and review score impact. Includes the patterns guests write about online but rarely raise with the front desk.

4

Guest Language Worth Reusing

Phrases real guests chose to describe the property - the most authentic copy available. Ready to use in OTA listings, social posts, and email subject lines.

5

Marketing Opportunities

Channel-by-channel suggestions grounded in what guests are actually saying. OTA listing changes, direct booking tactics, social content angles, email campaigns.

6

Suggested Website Copy

Draft headlines, property descriptions, and email copy written in the language guests already use. Not corporate speak - guest speak.

7

Suggested Review Responses

Drafted responses to representative positive and critical reviews - warm, specific, ready to personalize and publish.

8

Priority Action List

A ranked table: action, suggested owner, expected impact. Designed to move directly from this report into a team meeting agenda.
